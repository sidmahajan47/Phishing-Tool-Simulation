Phishing Awareness Tips:

  • Always verify the URL of websites you visit
  • Be cautious of login pages that look similar to legitimate ones
  • Check for SSL certificates (https://) before entering credentials
  • Never share your passwords or sensitive information through unofficial channels
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) whenever possible

How to Protect Yourself:

  • Use unique passwords for different accounts
  • Keep your software and systems updated
  • Use reliable antivirus software
  • Report suspicious activities to your IT department
